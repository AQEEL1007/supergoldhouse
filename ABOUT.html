About Super Gold House

Our Humble Beginnings

Super Gold House traces its roots back to the year 2000, when it first came to life on Wattegama Road, Ukuwela, under the name "Aqeel Jewelry Workshop." What started as a small workshop with only four dedicated members has grown into something truly remarkable. The original team consisted of Azwer, the founder, along with his brother Harees, Anas, and their cousin Haris.

A Journey of Growth

Over the span of 15 years, our skilled artisans honed their craft and created exceptional jewelry pieces. As time passed, the original team members pursued their individual paths, but the vision of creating exceptional jewelry endured. In 2015, Azwer set out on a journey abroad, acquiring invaluable experiences and insights in the world of jewelry.

The Birth of Super Gold House

In 2018, Azwer returned to Ukuwela, ready to take the next bold step. He transformed his vision into reality by opening a stunning jewelry showroom and a state-of-the-art workshop. It was then that Super Gold House was born. Today, Azwer not only leads as the sole owner but also stands as a seasoned jeweler, deeply passionate about creating exquisite pieces of jewelry.

Our Commitment to Excellence

At Super Gold House, we bring you a world of exceptional craftsmanship, creativity, and the finest jewelry. Our unwavering commitment to quality, meticulous attention to detail, and personalized service set us apart. Whether you're in search of breathtaking engagement rings, elegant necklaces, or unique custom designs, we are here to turn your dreams into reality.

Join Us on This Journey

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to everyone who has been a part of our journey. We invite you to explore our magnificent jewelry collection and experience the artistry that defines Super Gold House. Your presence and support mean the world to us, and we look forward to being a part of your cherished moments for years to come.
